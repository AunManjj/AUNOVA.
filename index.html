FAQ

Questions We Hear Often

What kind of brands does Aunova work with? +
We work best with product brands that already have evidence of demand — whether that's through their own store, Amazon, or retail. You don't need an existing creator presence, but you do need a product people genuinely want and margins that can support a performance-based model.
How is creator commerce different from influencer marketing? +
Traditional influencer marketing trades reach for brand awareness. Creator commerce is built entirely around conversion. Every creator we select, every piece of content we brief, and every dollar we allocate is tied directly to measurable revenue — not impressions or engagement rates.
We're already doing creator content but it's not working — can you help? +
This is exactly where Aunova excels. Stalled growth almost always comes down to one of three things: wrong creators, weak content briefs, or no amplification strategy. We diagnose which is true for your brand on the intro call and show you what a fix actually looks like.
When can we expect to see meaningful traction? +
The first batch of content typically goes live within two to three weeks of onboarding. Early revenue signals usually appear within the first month. By month two, you should have enough performance data to know exactly which creators and angles to double down on.
How involved do we need to be day-to-day? +
Minimal. You'll review and approve content before it goes live, and we'll have a weekly sync to walk through performance. Everything else — creator outreach, briefing, logistics, and optimization — is handled entirely by our team.
What makes you confident you can deliver? +
Every brand we've taken on has seen meaningful revenue growth within 90 days. That track record comes from a rigorous qualification process — we only take on brands where we genuinely believe we can win. If that's not the case for your brand, we'll tell you on the first call.
